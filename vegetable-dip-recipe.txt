Ingredients:
 - 1 pint of sour cream
 - 1 pint of Hellmann's Mayonaisse (use the sour cream container to measure)
 - 3 Tbsp dried onion flakes
 - 1 Tbsp dried parsley flakes
 - 1 tsp dried dill weed
 - 1 tsp (to taste) Lowry's Seasoned Salt

 Mix it all together, let sit for a while, then mix again.
 Serve with fresh vegetables.
