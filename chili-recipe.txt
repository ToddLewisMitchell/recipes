Ingredients:
 - 2 pounds ground beef
 - 2 cloves garlic, chopped
 - One 8-ounce can tomato sauce
 - One onion, chopped
 - 2 tablespoons chili powder
 - 2 tablespoons ground cumin
 - 1 teaspoon ground oregano
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
 - One 8-ounce can tomato paste
 - One 15-ounce can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
 - One 15-ounce can pinto beans, drained and rinsed

Directions:
 1. Brown the ground beef with onions in a large bot and drain off the excess fat.
 2. Add the tomato sauce, tomato paste, chili powder, cumin, oregano, salt, and cayenne.
 3. Add water to desired consistency.
 4. Stir, then cover and simmer over low heat for one hour.
 5. Add the beans and simmer for 10 more minutes.
